Waterproofing Services

Reliable and durable waterproofing solutions designed for industrial, commercial, and residential needs.

Roof Waterproofing

Protect your building from leaks, cracks, and heat with advanced roof waterproofing membranes and coatings.

  • High UV resistance
  • Durable coating systems
  • Suitable for concrete, metal & tiled roofs

Basement & Foundation Waterproofing

Prevent water ingress and protect structural integrity using advanced pressure-resistant waterproof membranes.

  • Prevents seepage & mold growth
  • Ideal for basements, retaining walls & foundations
  • Long-lasting protection

Bathroom & Terrace Waterproofing

Avoid leakage problems in bathrooms and terraces with flexible coatings that ensure complete water sealing.

  • High flexibility membranes
  • Crack-bridging and long life
  • Ideal for internal wet areas

Water Tank Waterproofing

Secure storage tanks and water retaining structures from leaks and bacterial growth with safe, non-toxic coatings.

  • Potable water safe coatings
  • Excellent adhesion to concrete
  • Long-term chemical resistance
